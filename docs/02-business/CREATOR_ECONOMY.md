# Kelza Creator Economy

> "Communities are built by people.
>
> Creators simply become the first person brave enough to start one."

---

# Purpose

The creator ecosystem is the heart of Kelza.

Users may install the application.

Creators give them reasons to stay.

Communities create belonging.

Belonging creates retention.

Retention creates sustainable business.

The objective is not to recruit influencers.

The objective is to recruit people who naturally create meaningful experiences for others.

---

# The Creator Problem

Today's creators often face similar challenges.

Algorithms decide visibility.

Communities are fragmented across platforms.

Income comes from multiple disconnected sources.

Relationships with audiences often depend on third-party platforms.

Creators spend more time chasing reach than building genuine communities.

Kelza aims to give creators a place where community matters more than algorithms.
