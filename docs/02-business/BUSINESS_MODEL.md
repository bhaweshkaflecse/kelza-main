# Kelza Business Model

> "Revenue is a by-product of creating value.
>
> The stronger the ecosystem becomes, the stronger the business becomes."

---

# Purpose

Kelza is a marketplace.

Not a social network.

Not a dating app.

Not a creator platform.

Not an AI application.

Kelza is a relationship marketplace where people, creators, communities and businesses exchange value.

Revenue is generated by enabling those exchanges.

The business grows as more meaningful interactions happen.

---

# Business Philosophy

Traditional platforms monetize attention.

Kelza monetizes value creation.

Instead of asking:

"How long can we keep users scrolling?"

We ask:

"How can we help people create meaningful experiences worth paying for?"

This creates healthier incentives for everyone.

---

Kelza is a two-sided marketplace.

Supply Side

↓

Creators

↓

Communities

↓

Experiences

↓

Demand Side

↓

Users

↓

Transactions

↓

Revenue

The stronger the supply side becomes, the healthier the marketplace becomes.

---

# Revenue Principles

Every revenue stream must satisfy three conditions.

1.

It creates real value.

2.

Both sides benefit.

3.

It strengthens the ecosystem.

If a revenue source weakens trust,

it should not exist.

---

# Revenue Streams

Kelza is intentionally diversified.

No single revenue source should dominate the business.

Version One focuses on a small number of proven revenue channels.

Future versions gradually expand the marketplace.

---

# V1 Revenue

## Private Sessions

Verified creators may offer:

• One-to-one video sessions

• One-to-one voice sessions

• Scheduled conversations

Kelza earns a marketplace commission.

---

## Digital Gifts

Users may send optional gifts.

Gifts are voluntary.

They represent appreciation.

Kelza earns a percentage of successful transactions.

---

## Creator Memberships

Creators may offer premium communities.

Members pay recurring subscriptions.

Kelza earns recurring marketplace revenue.

---

## Marketplace

Creators may sell:

Digital products

Community access

Tickets

Private experiences

Downloads

Future services

Kelza earns marketplace commissions.

# Why Users Pay

People do not pay for software.

They pay for outcomes.

Examples.

Conversation.

Entertainment.

Knowledge.

Recognition.

Belonging.

Access.

Events.

Learning.

Relationships.

Growth.

---

# Pricing Philosophy

Pricing should remain transparent.

Users should understand:

What they receive.

What they pay.

Why they pay.

Creators should understand:

How much they earn.

How commissions work.

How payouts work.

Trust begins with transparency.

---
