# Kelza Growth Engine

> "Growth is not something we buy.
>
> Growth is something we engineer."

---

# Purpose

Kelza is a marketplace.

Marketplaces cannot rely solely on paid advertising.

Long-term success comes from creating self-reinforcing growth loops where creators, communities and users continuously generate value for one another.

Paid acquisition accelerates the beginning.

The ecosystem drives the future.

---

# Growth Philosophy

We do not optimize for:

Downloads.

Installs.

Followers.

Views.

Vanity metrics.

We optimize for:

Healthy communities.

Active creators.

Returning users.

Meaningful conversations.

Marketplace activity.

Creator earnings.

Long-term retention.

Growth should always improve the ecosystem.

Never damage it.
