# Kelza Community System

> "People may join because of a creator.
>
> They stay because they become part of a community."

---

# Purpose

Communities are the foundation of Kelza.

Creators attract people.

Communities give those people a reason to stay.

Healthy communities generate:

Relationships.

Events.

Knowledge.

Entertainment.

Business opportunities.

Friendships.

Marketplace activity.

Long-term retention.

Communities are not features.

They are living ecosystems.

---

# Community Philosophy

A community is more than a chat room.

It is a shared identity.

People return because they feel:

Welcome.

Recognized.

Valued.

Safe.

Included.

Communities should create belonging.

Not simply discussion.
